\section{Creating Matrices}

Here's some matrices.

\[
\begin{matrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{matrix}
\]

\[
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{pmatrix}
\]

\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

\[
\begin{Bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{Bmatrix}
\]

\[
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{vmatrix}
\]

\[
\begin{Vmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{Vmatrix}
\]

A more interesting matrix that nests matrices:

\[
\begin{pmatrix}
\begin{matrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{matrix} & 0 \\
0 & \begin{matrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{matrix}
\end{pmatrix}
\]
